(IVCSM-2K20)


ABOUT US


The Department of Mechanical Engineering is one of the 7 engineering departments of KSR College of Engineering. The department provides both the UG and PG courses and is very active in teaching, research, consultancy and funding. The department has received Research Centre status from Anna University and offer research facilities for Welding, Dynamics of Machines, Fluid Mechanics, Materials Sciences and Mechatronics. The Mechanical Department provides educational base through active service of 42 faculty. Three copyrights have been received, two patents registered & 3 IPR applied and contributed in securing first place for KSRCE in filing patents among Anna University affiliated colleges consecutively for two years in a row
